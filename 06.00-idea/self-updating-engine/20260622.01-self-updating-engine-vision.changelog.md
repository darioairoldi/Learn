**Priority touch:** none · **Version bump:** major (initial → 1.0.0) · **P0 touched:** n/a (initial declaration)

**Initial vision — the portable, domain-agnostic self-updating engine that every self-updating domain instantiates with configuration rather than re-deriving the machinery.**

- Declared the engine's `scope.covers` (5 P0 capabilities: the Detect → Assess → Propose → Execute loop, the risk-calibrated autonomy gradient, change-risk classification, metadata-guarded changes, and the engine/integration seam; plus P1/P2 capabilities for cost-stratified assessment, processing state, graded verdict, invocation surface, domain-expertise injection, risk reduction, progressive learning, loop stability, and trust-calibrated adoption).
- Declared the `principles:` block (5 P0: `human-governed-autonomous-execution`, `risk-calibrated-autonomy`, `metadata-guarded-changes`, `staleness-avoidance-first`, `portable-by-design`; 8 P1; 2 P2), each with an inline rationale and `relies_on` references into the body rationale registry.
- Specified the **engine/integration seam** and the **domain-config contract** (identity / quality model / sources / thresholds), so a domain instantiates the engine without re-implementing machinery and without referencing another domain.
- Provenance: extracted from the mature prompt-engineering self-update system; motivated by [the extraction investigation](../../src/docs/90.%20Issues/202606/20260621.05-self-updating-machinery-extraction/overview.md). Migration of existing domains (prompt engineering, article writing) onto this engine is deferred to a future, owner-made decision.
